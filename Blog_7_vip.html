VIP INC SELECTS RAILCARRX TO STREAMLINE ITS CAR REPAIR BILLING PROCESS

Dallas, TX and Sarnia, Ontario: Vidal Street Industrial Park Inc (known as VIP) announced the implementation of RailcarRx’s Repair Management System (RailcarRx RMS™), a cloud-based solution to manage their Car Repair Billing process and shop operation. As a leading storage, repair and transloading facility, VIP needed an efficient system that would help them automate and streamline their repair operation, especially related to car repair billing and inventory management. Prior to RailcarRx RMS, VIP relied on several time-consuming manual processes that involved maintaining data in multiple spreadsheets. With RailcarRx RMS, they can now track and store car information, maintain inventory, manage their yard and generate industry required standard billing formats all through one platform.

“We used to generate car repair billing manually and it no longer met our needs. RailcarRx’s system is very easy-to-use and renders the invoicing and repair process more efficient than before. It enables us to provide our customers a better service,” says Brian McEachran, the director of VIP Inc.

Repair shops that enter bills on the TTX SEI Portal can now utilize RailcarRx to create and manage repairs rather than perform double entry of data between systems.

“Josh Lippy, Senior Director of Business Development at RailcarRx says “VIP outgrew its car repair billing process very quickly, RailcarRx RMS has helped by combining many disparate processes into one complete platform thereby streamlining their operations.”

About Vidal Street Industrial Park Inc:

VIP Inc is one of Canada’s largest railcar storage, repair and transload providers. With over 1000 railcar storage capacity, car cleaning expertise and valve repair certified, VIP is offering more value added services than ever before. For customers who need products transloaded from or to truck, VIP is an ideal Mid-Western location. Found in the center of Sarnia’s chemical valley, they are directly south of CN’s yard and the tunnel connecting Ontario to Michigan and only a day’s drive from over one hundred and twenty five thousand Americans located from Chicago to New York. They specialize in transloading a myriad of different products from high purity to Hazardous Mat safely and accurately. VIP is the place to store, clean, repair and load tank cars. For more information visit www.viprail.com.
