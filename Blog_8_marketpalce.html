RailcarRx Launches Marketplace to Connect Railcar Repair Shops to Equipment Owners

DALLAS, TEXAS 11/10/2017: RailcarRx, Inc. announces the launch of RailcarRx Marketplace™ ( www.marketplace.railcarrx.com), a web-based platform to connect railcar repair shops to equipment owners. Today, Marketplace allows railcar repair shops to register and post their locations, services, capacity, and contact information. Equipment owners can easily lookup shops by geographic locations.

RailcarRx Marketplace™ was created to support the digital decision-making process of shopping repair services. The platform is designed for railcar owners to find ideal repair services based on a railcars’ location. It provides equipment owners a list of repair shops based on customizable search criteria. Each shop has its own profile page which they can easily update with key information regarding their shop(s).

"Since its launch on September 20 th this year, RailcarRx Marketplace has almost 30 repair shops with multiple locations included on the site, and the number keeps growing. We are offering RailcarRx Marketplace to repair shops and equipment owners at no charge hoping to establish an open and beneficial platform for the entire industry. In the future we plan to include more segments from the rail industry, such as parts suppliers, car manufacturers and so on,” says Josh Lippy, the Director of Operations at RailcarRx.

If you are a repair shop and would like to add your information, simply go to www.marketplace.railcarrx.com and click the blue “add your shop” button on the top right corner. In four easy steps, your shop will be available on Marketplace for the railcar owners to find.

About RailcarRx: RailcarRx is a technology solution provider for repair shops, railcar owners and railroads in North America. They specialize in out-of-the-box and custom cloud-based solutions in the rail industry. Some of the largest players in the industry utilize RailcarRx solutions to optimize revenue and improve efficiency. For more information visit http://www.railcarrx.com.
