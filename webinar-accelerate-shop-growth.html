Webinar Overview:

How two of the top ten equipment leasing companies choose repair shops and evaluate ongoing business relationships?

Here are the top 3 takeaways of the webinar:

1. Quick turnaround is the key for car owners to measure repair shops' performance. Typically 30 days is an ideal turnaround time for car owners.

2. Shops can provide quotes and order materials ahead of a piece of equipment’s arrival to improve the turnaround time.

3. Car owners expect on-time and accurate information from the shops such as repair status, POD, and standardized billing, which can be improved by utilizing systems and technology.
