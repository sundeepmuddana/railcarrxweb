RailcarRx:: Analytics in Action
Accelerating Productivity through Data Analytics. The RailcarRx Analytics team works with Rail Industry clients to develop and deploy analytics platforms.

You have the data – now let us help you use it to make better decisions . . .

We work with railroads and railcar owners to build the framework and tools needed to make informed, forward-looking decisions aimed at improving operational efficiencies and reducing costs. By utilizing RailcarRx’s Analytics in Action approach our clients can unlock, distill and visualize relevant and actionable data for improving operating and business processes including:

  • Safety and inspection processes

    Railcar, locomotive and rail maintenance cycles

    Fuel and labor cost management

  • Traffic, scheduling (e.g. bad stops) and rail network management

    Environmental and health management actions

    Financial systems

Data without context, is just data

Even though there are significant operating similarities across the Rail industry, there are also distinct and unique operating processes and approaches for each organization. For many in the industry this is the competitive edge. The science of data management is built around first understanding business problems, objectives and key performance indicators. RailcarRx Analytics Data Scientists are skilled at working with each client to understand what questions and issues are important in effectively managing day-over-day and predictively, for the future. Those questions, if answered with a high degree of confidence, driven by actionable data, will give you the opportunity to make better decisions. Now, imagine if those answers also were presented to you in a format that would allow for those “better decisions” faster and with more accuracy. That is RailcarRx Analytics in Action.
