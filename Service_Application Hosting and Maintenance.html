Application Hosting and
Maintenance Services (AHM)

Improve the efficiency of IT operations, customer support and
software application maintenance.

The AHM team at RailcarRx helps IT departments keep their internal software development teams focused on new application development while RailcarRx supports ongoing hosting and maintenance for existing applications. For other customers, RailcarRx AHM is a good alternative to retaining expensive IT staff resources in-house to support applications in production.

  • Reduce internal staff overhead costs for application/systems maintenance

    Improve support for ongoing bug fixes and feature requests with a RailcarRx dedicate support team

  • Allocate core FTE technical resources to critical software development projects – RailcarRx will handle the maintenance for existing applications

    Ensure application “uptime” and established service level agreements (SLA’s)
