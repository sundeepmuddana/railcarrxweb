Data Analytics in Action Services

RailcarRx Data Analytics in Action Services helps you accelerate operational productivity by distilling actionable data
into clear, enterprise-wide management platforms.

You Have the Data – Now Let Us Help You Use It to Make Better Decisions . . .

We work with managers in the Rail Industry to build the frameworks and tools needed to make informed, forward-looking decisions aimed at improving operational efficiencies and reducing costs. By utilizing RailcarRx’s Data Analytics in Action Services, our clients can unlock, distill and visualize relevant and actionable data for improving operating and business processes including:

  • Safety and inspection processes

    Railcar, locomotive and rail maintenance cycles

    Fuel and labor cost management

  • Traffic, scheduling (e.g. bad stops) and rail network management

    Environmental and health management actions

    Financial systems

Data Without Context, is Just Data...

Even though there are significant operating similarities across the Rail industry, there are also distinct and unique operating processes and approaches for each organization. For many in the industry this truly is the competitive edge. RailcarRx Analytics team works closely with our client managers to understand their unique business problems, management objectives and key performance indicators. RailcarRx Analytics Data Scientists are skilled at working with each client to understand what questions and issues are important in effectively managing day-over-day and predictively, for the future. Those questions, if answered with a high degree of confidence, driven by actionable data, highlights opportunities to make better decisions.
