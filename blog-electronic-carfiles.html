Better Prepare for FRA Inspriation and Customer Communications

An unexpected FRA inspection can be stressful and worrisome when it occurs. Compound that with customers requesting forms, images of equipment damage, and revised estimates and invoices, and you have yourself a real record keeping nightmare.

In the “good old days” repair shops used to print out and keep records for each piece of equipment they repaired. I have heard stories of “rows of filing cabinets” filled with these files. RailcarRx Repair Management System completely eliminates the need print documents or save them in special files on your computer. The “Electronic Car File” is a unique feature that has been helping our customers keep everything organized for each repair instance. In fact, the system keeps them so organized that the stress of an FRA inspection is negated when it comes to the record keeping portions. Customers love it too, as documents, forms, images, and each and every version of the estimate and invoice is saved inside the system for easy access and sharing with customers.

How to better prepare for FRA’s visit with the Electronic Car Files

In 2016 the Federal Railroad Administration (FRA) reported the highest-ever civil penalty collection rate in the agency’s 50-year history. We haven’t seen a year-over-year comparison for 2017 yet, but we can assume the FRA is headed in a similar direction.

*Source: https://www.fra.dot.gov/eLib/details/L18477

There is no doubt that repair shops always need to be well-prepared for an FRA inspection. According to some of our customers, having all the paperwork and documentation in place is the #1 rule when an inspection occurs.

The documentation, as our customers suggest, that you should have include:

  • Quality Assurance Manual.
  • C3 Manual.
  • Procedures forms.
  • Equipment calibration logs.
  • Equipment data files.
  • AAR Field Manual.

RailcarRx RMS makes the preparation so easy because it keeps everything organized in one place. All the information related to equipment has been stored in the Electronic Car Files. The files include paperwork from arrival through invoicing for each piece of equipment at a very detailed level, no matter if the equipment has been repaired or is currently under repair in the facility. Additionally, you can upload customer emails, MSDS sheets, images, car owner specs, forms, and any other information that may be required for you to provide to the FRA to the Electronic Car File. RailcarRx RMS also provides the ability to print these documents in bulk if an inspector should arrive onsite.


How the Electronic Car Files help improve your customers’ experience

Just like preparing for FRA inspection, communicating with customers is a similar process. Providing supporting documents, images, estimates and invoices can be a full time job depending on how much equipment is passing through your shop.

The reason why RailcarRx RMS users love the Electronic Car Files is because it better enables communication with their customers and backs up all the repair history and information in one place. It simply makes their daily work life much easier.

The system enables you and your team to pull out all the information your customers need with just a couple of clicks. Whenever your customer have questions about the equipment’s repair status or other information, you can impress them by providing the accurate and detailed answers quickly. It even allows you to send them directly from RailcarRx RMS to your customer, no more downloading and attaching files to emails.

At RailcarRx, we constantly take feedback from our users and strive to make our systems as user-friendly and efficient as possible.
