What RailcarRx Insight® Can Do for You

With RailcarRx Insight®, railcar owners and shippers can optimize railcar maintenance and reduce repair costs, obtain a bird’s eye view of the entire railcar fleet, access industry data, monitor equipment health, and assess risk based on a configurable rules engine.

Improve Visibility of
Entire Railcar Fleet

RailcarRx Insight manages fleet in key aspects and offers real-time business insights by providing several pre-defined reports including Car Info Report, Historial Car Report, Car Financial Report, Leasing Info Report, etc.

Identify High-risk Equipment
Plan Maintenance Proactively

The system integrates with Rail Industry Data and also allows you to set up your own fleet management rules to identify high-risk equipment to make proactive maintenance plans and save costs.

Support Shopping Decision
Save Repair Costs

Insight supports your shopping decisions by showing you the shops at your choice and other shops nearby railcars’ destination. It provides a cost comparison report with the cost difference between AAR repairs, contract shop repairs and mobile repairs.

Streamline Shopping Process
Better Communicate with Shops

Once the repair and shopping decisions are made, Insights tracks the repair status of your railcars at a detailed level and sends you notifications on updates. You can grand shops the access to the system through shops’ portal to better communicate with them directly through Insight.

Track Railcar Fleet
Validate Demurrage Costs

Insight's Full Tracking Dashboard monitors your railcar fleet by integrating with CLM data to track railcars' locations, dwell & cycles times and provides detailed information about bad-ordered equipment, free-runners, not-moving railcars. It also supports to better plan yard capacity and to validate demurrage charges.

Audit BRC Invoices
Save Time and Cost

After receiving invoices from repair shops, Insight's BRC Auditing Feature identifies and highlights the errors on the invoices by checking with standard AAR auditing rules and specific user-configurable auditing rules. It saves your time and spots the invoice errors right away.

Turn Insights into Cost-saving Decisions

A railcar owner and Insight user reported based on a small sample of their total fleet: a reduction in their monthly AAR billing from $100,000 to $20,000 and AAR repairs have stabilized.
