Application Modernization Project Services

Enhance, integrate and align . . .
Offering cost-effective solutions to improved application functionality, modernization and ongoing cost of ownership.

From projects involving the creation of a functional integration framework between legacy and newer applications, to the development of a flexible “open source” presentation layer for users on the front-end or even a complete application re-write, RailcarRx can help. Projects Commonly involving our Application Upgrade Services may include:

Rewrite and Tuning

Applications built in legacy code or those operating on unsupported platforms are upgraded within the same technology stack

Integration

Legacy applications that require integration from their current state to a single user interface (UI) and with other back-end systems

Conversion

Moving data and functionality of applications to an entirely new enterprise platform (SAP, Oracle or other 3rd party applications)

How We Can Help With Your Company

To help lower the high costs involved in integrating legacy applications with today’s more advanced enterprise environments, RailcarRx maintains experienced resources, locally and off-shore giving our clients an ability to address application modernization projects more efficiently.
