Josh Lippy

Director of Operations

Josh Lippy has been working in the transportation industry for 9 years. In 2010 Josh joined Railinc, a subsidiary of the Association of American Railroads. Josh served in various roles at Railinc including Business Development to partner with equipment owners, shippers, and intermodal shippers to provide track & trace solutions. In 2013 Josh accepted a role as Industry Relations Director managing data between the largest equipment owners and Railinc. Josh joined RailCarRx in 2015 to help manage and grow its solutions
