Data Analytics

In addition to our Data Analytics practice, RailcarRx provides Data Scientists on an individual consulting basis who are skilled at working with each Rail Industry client to understand what metrics are important in effectively managing their business processes and assist in the development and delivery of effective decision support systems.

Positive Train Control

RailcarRx provides expert technical resources that have experience supporting Positive Train Control (PTC) systems development and implementation initiatives. Part of the challenge in implementing a successful PTC system lies in the complexity in integrating thousands of components relaying critical information across a network that may include a range of communications technologies including cellular, Wi-Fi, GPS and radio signals.
