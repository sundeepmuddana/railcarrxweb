About RailcarRx

RailcarRx is a leading IT provider of software products and services with numerous years of expertise in custom software development for companies in the rail industry. RailcarRx’s first software product RailcarRx Repair Management System was built in 2014 and officially launched in October of the same year. RailcarRx INSIGHT & RailcarRx CRB were launched in the following years. We have successfully grown our customer base and worked to continually improve our systems to make them more user-friendly and feature-rich than any other similar offerings in the market. We are committed to excellence in our performance and individualized approach for each customer. We believe that every company is unique and aims to deliver a product that can best suit each client’s needs. We strive to continuously listen to our customers and provide exceptional service after the sales.
